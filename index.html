About Us

We make everything from 'farsans' to traditional snacks and dishes. Throughout our journey of 5 years, we have provided services of utmost satisfaction to our customers. Our motto has always been 'Quality is non negotiable', hence we strive to provide you the best possible and good quality service. We make everything at home itself to give you the very homely taste.

Note : WE ONLY ACCEPT FRESH TO EAT ORDERS

Why are we so special?

Hygienic but Delicious

We bring the best from our home to provide you delicacies that are of great quality. Our dishes are full of hygiene combined with the richness of taste. We use less oil and artificial ingredients, rather we focus on natural 'masalas' and herbs.

Experience

We have been running this bussiness for the last 5 years. Throughout this journey we have provided we have acquired orders of all types, large or small. Our expertise in fresh to make orders has ensured storng word of mouth in our favour.
